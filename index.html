Their Story

Under new ownership since December 2019

The Catio was founded as a haven for rescued cats while offering a vibrant and social environment for all hoomans!
"All of our cats and kittens have been rescued by our partners at Nashville Cat Rescue. Each Catio kitty resident spends their days socializing with our guests during kitty time and our wide variety of events. This priceless enrichment allows the cats‚Äô personalities to blossom before they find forever homes.
Recently, we've expanded our offerings with the addition of the Senior Catio, providing a dedicated space for older cats seeking companionship and a loving home.
